Ingredients

  • The Rice itself
  • water
  • salt

Steps

    Finally, the recipe!
  1. Measure a desired amount of rice in a container
  2. Use that same container to measure the water to be used. Put two full containers of water in whatever you are going to cook in
  3. Add the measured rice to the measured water
  4. Add an appropriate amount of salt
  5. boil the mixture until all the water evapourates
